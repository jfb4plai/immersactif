Audio ambience layers for the sensory scene.

whispers.wav, corridor.wav, chairs.wav, bell.wav are PROCEDURALLY GENERATED by
scripts/generate-audio.mjs (no external source, public domain by construction).

They are abstract sensory textures, not field recordings. To use real recordings
later, replace these files with CC0 loops of the same names — the app loads
public/audio/<name>.wav unchanged.
